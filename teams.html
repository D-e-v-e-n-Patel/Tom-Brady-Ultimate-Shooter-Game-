Career on the New England Patriots


As the team's primary starter for 18 seasons, Brady led the Patriots to 17 division titles (including 11 consecutive from 2009 to 2019), 13 AFC Championship Games (including eight consecutive from 2011 to 2018), nine Super Bowl appearances, and six Super Bowl titles, all NFL records for a player and franchise.

Career on the Tampa Bay Buccaneers

Brady signed with the Tampa Bay Buccaneers on March 20, 2020. The two-year contract was worth $50 million in fully guaranteed money, with up to $4.5 million each year in incentives. Brady brought his team to become the champions of Super Bowl XXXVl in 2021. This was his first year playing for the team. During his second year they lost in the divisional round against the Super Bowl winning team of the year, the LA Rams.
