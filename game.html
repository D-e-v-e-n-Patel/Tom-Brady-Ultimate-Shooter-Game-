How to Play


To play the "Tom Brady Ultimate Shooter game" is very simple; you just need to used the w, a, s, d keys to move left, right, up and down. This allows you move around the field and allows you to evade incoming enemies. The last ting you need to use to play is a mouse to shoot a football. This allows you to hit the enemies to stop them from tackling you.

Hints

Hold down the clicker on your mouse and aim down to shoot many footballs in a row
Always keep an eye on where enemies are coming from
Tom Brady has a cool sound effect when he hits an enemy with a football.
